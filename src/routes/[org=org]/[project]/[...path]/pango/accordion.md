title: Accordion
description: A list inside !!! accordion becomes a group of collapsible rows. Opening one closes the others (native exclusive details).
---

Click a question, and the previously open one closes:

!!! accordion
    - **How do I reset my key?**

      Open Settings, then Security, then Reset. A fresh key is emailed to you.

    - **Where are logs stored?**

      Under `~/.local/state/pango/`, rotated weekly.

    - **Can Pango work offline?**

      Yes. He hoards ants and runs without a network.
